Given a lecture PDF, produce a **derivation-focused lecture scribe**.

Goal:
Enable exam revision by reconstructing how every important formula and result is derived.

Rules:
- Only include content from the lecture (no external additions).
- For every formula:
  - Show its origin (definition, assumption, or previous result)
  - Derive it step-by-step
  - Clearly state dependencies
- Preserve original notation and symbols exactly.
- Include proofs exactly as presented, with all logical steps.
- Include examples only if present in the lecture.
- Avoid intuition, storytelling, or simplification.
- Keep the writing dense, structured, and exam-ready.

Structure:
- Notation & Preliminaries
- Core Definitions
- Step-by-Step Derivations (with source of each formula)
- Proofs (complete and ordered)
- Examples (if present)
- Final Formula Sheet (derived results only)

Do not explain beyond what is strictly necessary for reconstruction.
